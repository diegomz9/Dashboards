# U.S. Market Performance Dashboard Power BI

## Overview
This project analyzes the market performance of 10 major U.S. companies using Power BI, focusing on returns, trading volume, price behavior, volatility, and sector dynamics over 2025 period.

The goal of this dashboard is not only to visualize financial data, but to extract actionable insights that explain why certain companies outperformed others and how investor behavior differed across sectors.

---

## Business Questions
- Which companies delivered the strongest returns, and were those returns supported by trading activity?
- How does investor attention (volume) relate to performance?
- How do tech stocks vs consumer staples compare in terms of growth, volatility, and stability?
- What seasonal patterns emerge across the year?

---

## Tools & Skills Used
- **Power BI**
  - DAX
  - Power Query (data cleaning & transformation)
  - Interactive visuals, slicers and titles. 
- **Data Analysis**
- **Data Storytelling**
